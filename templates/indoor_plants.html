Indoor Plants

Snake Plant

Easy to care for, tolerates low light, and improves indoor air quality.

Care Tips:

  • Water sparingly, allow soil to dry between waterings.
  • Thrives in indirect sunlight.
  • Keep away from pets.

Peace Lily

Beautiful white flowers, purifies air, and easy to maintain.

Care Tips:

  • Keep soil moist but not soggy.
  • Prefers low to medium light.
  • Wipe leaves to remove dust.

Spider Plant

Great for hanging baskets, air purifier, and easy to grow.

Care Tips:

  • Water moderately, allow soil to dry slightly between waterings.
  • Thrives in bright, indirect light.
  • Remove dead leaves regularly.

Fiddle Leaf Fig

Large, violin-shaped leaves, adds a dramatic touch to any room.

Care Tips:

  • Water when top inch of soil is dry.
  • Prefers bright, indirect light.
  • Keep away from drafts.

ZZ Plant

Low maintenance, tolerates low light and drought.

Care Tips:

  • Water every 2-3 weeks, allow soil to dry out.
  • Thrives in low to bright indirect light.
  • Wipe leaves occasionally.

Aloe Vera

Succulent with medicinal properties, easy to grow indoors.

Care Tips:

  • Water deeply but infrequently.
  • Needs bright, indirect sunlight.
  • Use well-draining soil.

Rubber Plant

Glossy leaves, adds greenery and purifies air.

Care Tips:

  • Water when topsoil is dry.
  • Prefers bright, indirect light.
  • Wipe leaves to keep shiny.

Pothos (Money Plant)

Trailing vine, easy to grow, great for shelves and hanging baskets.

Care Tips:

  • Water when soil feels dry.
  • Thrives in low to bright indirect light.
  • Trim to maintain shape.

Philodendron

Heart-shaped leaves, easy to care for, great indoor plant.

Care Tips:

  • Water when top inch of soil is dry.
  • Prefers medium to bright indirect light.
  • Keep away from pets.

Monstera

Large, split leaves, tropical look, popular indoor plant.

Care Tips:

  • Water when top 2 inches of soil are dry.
  • Thrives in bright, indirect light.
  • Keep leaves clean and dust-free.

Boston Fern

Lush, feathery foliage, adds greenery and humidity.

Care Tips:

  • Keep soil consistently moist.
  • Prefers indirect light and high humidity.
  • Trim dead fronds regularly.

Chinese Evergreen

Variegated leaves, tolerant of low light and neglect.

Care Tips:

  • Water when top inch of soil is dry.
  • Thrives in low to medium light.
  • Wipe leaves occasionally.

Jade Plant

Succulent with thick, shiny leaves, symbolizes good luck.

Care Tips:

  • Water sparingly, allow soil to dry between waterings.
  • Needs bright light.
  • Use well-draining soil.

Calathea

It has vibrant foliage with stunning patterns.The leaves close at night.

Care Tips:

  • Keep soil moist but not waterlogged.
  • Prefers medium to low indirect light.
  • High humidity is essential; mist regularly.

Anthurium

Tropical plant with glossy leaves and vibrant, waxy flowers.

Care Tips:

  • Water when soil feels dry to the touch.
  • Prefers bright, indirect light.
  • Maintain high humidity and warm temperatures.

Parlor Palm

Graceful, arching fronds; ideal for low-light environments.

Care Tips:

  • Water when the top layer of soil dries out.
  • Tolerates low to medium indirect light.
  • Use well-draining, peat-based soil.

Alocasia Polly

Dramatic plant with arrow-shaped, dark green leaves and striking white veins.

Care Tips:

  • Keep soil consistently moist but not soggy.
  • Requires bright, indirect light.
  • High humidity is essential; mist regularly.

Areca Palm

Elegant feathery fronds; excellent natural humidifier and air purifier.

Care Tips:

  • Water when the topsoil starts to dry.
  • Prefers bright, indirect light.
  • Mist leaves regularly to maintain humidity.
